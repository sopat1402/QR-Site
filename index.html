How to use this site?

The row of options in orange are used to select the kind of content you wish to convert to a QR. Then, type the content into the white textbox and click the orange button that says generate QR. You can then scan the QR directly or download it as a PNG.

To scan a QR simply hold it in the camera view of your smartphone and it usually auto detects a QR and shows options for what you can do with it.

Features of the site

URL

Convert a URL into a QR code which when scans says open link.

Phone number

Opens the dialer with the phone number pre loaded when the QR is scanned.

SMS

Scanning the QR pre loads the phone's SMS feature to send a message to the encoded number.

Text

When the QR is scanned, encoded text is simply displayed to the user.
